How do I apply to work with Influvise?

Simply apply for an invite through our website, and we'll review your profile to see if we align with your brand vision.

How many creators do you work with at a time?

We only work with a limited number of creators—no more than 10 at a time—ensuring a personalized, high-quality experience for each.

What's the process once I'm accepted?

Once accepted, we'll schedule an exclusive strategy session to plan your merch, followed by design, production, and launch—handled entirely by us.

Can I have complete creative control over my merch?

Absolutely. We collaborate with you to ensure your merch is 100% aligned with your vision, but we handle all the technical details.

How long does it take to launch my merch?

The timeline varies, but from strategy session to launch, we aim to have your merch ready within a few weeks, depending on design and production specifics.
